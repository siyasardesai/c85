My school name is sant karens high school

Hi!

The Patron Saint of the school is St. Katherine of Siena. Her feast day is on the 29th of April. This is celebrated as St. Karen’s Day. The school has derived its name from the novel KAREN written by Marie Lyons Killilea. ‘KAREN’ is the inspiring true story of a little girl who triumphed over disaster and her delightful family who made her triumph possible. Imbued with the same spirit of sacrifice, love, devotion and care for children, St. Karen’s was established.
